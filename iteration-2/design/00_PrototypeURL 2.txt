For the clickable paper prototype we decided on using Figma instead of Marvel. The result is the same: We scanned the paper prototypes, uploaded them to Figma, and created click- and drag-able areas for the interaction (Swipe-Tab allows swiping the cards to the left for the first card and to the right for the following two cards).
